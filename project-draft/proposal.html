Project Proposal

Proposal for a workout recommendation website

Problem Description

Our goal is to develop a website for fitness, which provides workout recommendations for its user and helps the user keep up with the schedule. Workout at home is challenging for several reasons.

  1. People lack knowledge of what to do and how to do it. We are drowning in information. We can get millions of answers to anything in nanoseconds. We know that we should exercise, but we may not be comfortable trying something we saw in a magazine without some more guidance or experience.
  2. People don't have a workout goal. This goes beyond the goal of, 'I want to lose weight'. This problem arises when you can't answer the following sample questions; “What body parts am I going to work on?”, “Am I going to do intervals? If so, what's my work to rest ratio going to be today? What was it last week?”, “What did I do last time, and do I need to switch things up or stay the same to reach my goals this week/month/etc?”

Existing attempt to solve this problem includes:

  • Apple Fitness Plus
  • Sweat

These sites are great, but they are subscription-based. We hope to build a website that provides detailed information for people who want to work out at home with only open and free resources on the Internet. We hope to help everyone achieve their ideal body shape.

Proposed Application

The website is mainly for users that chase fitness habits. There are a lot of people who want to start a fitness or work out. However, they do not know how to start and what to do. Because fitness could be potentially dangerous, clarifications about some common misconceptions are needed.

The data will be a CSV file and links to professional videos or passages for people to view.

The common misconception will place on the website. The website will be divided into different sections, and people can click the section to find the body part that they want to work out. There will be a calculator for the user to use as a body statue.

The application is basically guidance for people who are just starting to work out or want to start a workout. With this guidance, people can just follow the order on the website and access the right video to learn the correct knowledge.
